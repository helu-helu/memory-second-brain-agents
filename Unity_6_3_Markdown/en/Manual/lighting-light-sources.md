# Light sources

Resources for adding light to a **scene**A Scene contains the environments and menus of your game. Think of each unique Scene file as a unique level. In each Scene, you place your environments, obstacles, and decorations, essentially designing and building your game in pieces. [More info](CreatingScenes.html)  
See in [Glossary](Glossary.html#Scene), with Light components, **ambient light**Light that doesn’t come from any specific direction, and contributes equal light in all directions to the Scene. [More info](lighting-window.html)
