A particle system can display and move particles in great numbers to represent a fluid or amorphous entity. The effect of all the particles together creates the impression of the complete entity, such as smoke. [More info](class-ParticleSystem.html)  
See in [Glossary](Glossary.html#particle) Systems emit particles, and the properties of individual particles from a **Particle System**A component that simulates fluid entities such as liquids, clouds and flames by generating and animating large numbers of small 2D images in the scene.
