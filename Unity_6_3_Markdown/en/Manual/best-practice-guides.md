Learn about importing and exporting animations, **humanoid animations**An animation using humanoid skeletons. Humanoid models generally have the same basic structure, representing the major articulate parts of the body, head and limbs. This makes it easy to map animations from one humanoid skeleton to another, allowing retargeting and inverse kinematics. [More info](ConfiguringtheAvatar.html)  
See in [Glossary](Glossary.html#Humanoidanimation), shortcuts, animating UI, events, and more.
